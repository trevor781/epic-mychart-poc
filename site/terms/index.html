What this app does

Personal Health lets you retrieve your own health records from your health system using Epic's patient-access FHIR APIs. You authorize access by logging in with your own patient portal (MyChart) credentials. Access is read-only: the app cannot add to, change, or delete anything in your medical record.

Your data

All retrieved health data is stored only on your own device. It is not transmitted to, stored on, or processed by any server operated by this app. Your data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party. Your portal password is never seen by the app; authentication happens directly with your health system.

Revoking access

You can revoke this app's access at any time from your MyChart account settings (Security & Account Settings → Linked Apps and Devices), or by contacting your health system.

No medical advice

This app displays your health records for informational purposes only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider about your health.
